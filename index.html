About Me

I am Shahajas Thekkan, an Indian professional footballer born on 25 October 1998 in Perinthalmanna, Kerala, India. I primarily play as a right back and stand at 1.75 m (5 ft 9 in) tall.

I began my youth career with Baselius CC and later moved to Ozone FC before joining Kerala Blasters FC's reserve side in 2017. I was part of the Kerala Premier League-winning squad in the 2019–20 season and was promoted to the senior team in 2021.

Career Highlights
